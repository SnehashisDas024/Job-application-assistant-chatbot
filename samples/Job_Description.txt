JOB TITLE: Senior Product Designer
COMPANY: NovaFinancial Tech
LOCATION: New York, NY (Hybrid)

ABOUT THE ROLE:
We are looking for a Senior Product Designer to lead the design of our next-gen cryptocurrency exchange platform. You will work closely with Product Managers and Engineers to translate complex financial data into clean, intuitive user interfaces.

RESPONSIBILITIES:
* Lead the end-to-end design process from user research to high-fidelity prototyping.
* Create and maintain a comprehensive Design System using Figma.
* Conduct usability testing and A/B testing to improve conversion rates and user retention.
* Collaborate with frontend developers to ensure design feasibility (knowledge of React is a plus).
* Mentor junior designers and advocate for UX best practices across the organization.

REQUIREMENTS:
* 5+ years of experience as a UX/UI or Product Designer, preferably in FinTech or Banking.
* Expert proficiency in Figma (Auto-layout, Components, Variables) is mandatory.
* Strong portfolio demonstrating mobile-first design and complex data visualization.
* Experience with user research methodologies (personas, journey mapping).
* Basic understanding of HTML/CSS is required; JavaScript knowledge is a huge plus.
* Excellent communication skills to present design rationale to stakeholders.

NICE TO HAVE:
* Experience with Blockchain/Web3 technologies.
* Experience working in an Agile/Scrum environment.
